Exploring similar words by cosine distance

The following scatter plot shows the sentences available in our training data in 2 dimensional space. The histograms on the right have bins of cosine distance between input questions and the above mentioned sentences. The height of each bins represents the number of questions lying in that particular range. You can click on them to see those sentences on the scatter plot.
